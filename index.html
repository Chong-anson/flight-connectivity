Welcome to Flight Connectivity!

A visualization of airports' connectivity.

There are two digrams, Force and Arc.

Force digram

Each node represents an airport, while an edge means there is a direct route between these airports. The color of the node is determined by its country, where airports in the same country have the same color.

Distance between connected edges is determined by their flight time. Note that this will sort of group the nodes by its continent !

By default, it shows the connectivity between Top 50 Busiest Airports around the globe. You can also choose airports to display as you wish!

  • Hover over an airport to show its connectivity with others.
  • Click on an airport to display airport's info.
  • Drag an airport to see how it interacts with other nodes!

Arc diagram

It sorts the order of Top 20 airports as you wish!

  • Hover over an airport to show its connectivity with others.
