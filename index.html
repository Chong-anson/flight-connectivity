Flight Connectivity Instructions!

It is a visualization of airports' connectivity.

There are two digrams, Force and Arc.

For Force digram, each nodes represent an airport, while an edge means there is a direct rounte between these airports. By default, it shows the connectivity between Top 50 Busiest Airports around the globe. You can also choose airports to display as you wish!

  • Hover over an airport to show its connectivity with others.
  • Click on an airport to display airport's info.
  • Drag an airport to see how it interacts with other nodes!
For Arc diagram, it sorts the order of Top 20 airports as you wish! Hover over an airport to show its connectivity with others.
